A few applications...

Permeability in geomaterials

Neutron imaging is ideal to explore te effect of localisation on permeability of gematerials.

Hydraulic fracturing

Neutrons can pass through thick metal casings and are an ideal tool to image water even in thin cracks

Concrete behaviour at high temperatures

Key aspects of this phenomenon, such as water and gas content are an ideal application of Neutrons.

Flow of water along concrete

Crucial for the durability of buildings, neutrons can directly and non-detructively observe this phenomenon.

Humidity propagation in fibrous media

Of great interest for paper manifacturing

Bio-fil monitoring in soils

Of great interest for water filtration
